Void Dominion — Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 28, 2026

You may request the deletion of your Void Dominion account and all associated data. Once processed, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What will be deleted

Upon processing your request, we will delete all data associated with your account, including your player profile, gameplay progression, commander roster, purchase history records, chat history, and analytics data linked to your account.

What will NOT be affected

Purchase records held by Google Play or the Apple App Store are managed by those platforms and are not affected by this request. You may contact those platforms directly regarding their records.

Submit a Deletion Request

To request deletion of your account and data, please email us at:

privacy@voidgaming.com

Include the subject line "Data Deletion Request — Void Dominion" and, if possible, your Player ID (found in Settings within the game).

Processing Time

We will process your request within 30 days of receipt and confirm deletion via the email address you contacted us from.

Contact

If you have questions about this process, contact us at privacy@voidgaming.com.
