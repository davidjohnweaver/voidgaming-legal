Void Dominion — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Void Gaming ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile game Void Dominion ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account or sign in, we collect your authentication credentials through Unity Authentication services. This may include a unique player identifier.

Gameplay Data: We collect gameplay analytics such as session duration, progression milestones, feature usage, and in-game economy interactions to improve the game experience.

Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction records are processed by the platform store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We receive confirmation of purchases but do not directly collect or store payment card details.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and general performance metrics to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

Provide, maintain, and improve the Game; process in-app purchases and deliver purchased content; analyze gameplay patterns to improve balance and player experience; communicate with you about updates or support requests; and comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Unity Gaming Services: Authentication, analytics, and cloud save functionality. See Unity's Privacy Policy.

Google Play / Apple App Store: Purchase processing and distribution. See their respective privacy policies.

Vivox: Voice and text communication services. See Unity's Privacy Policy (Vivox is a Unity service).

4. Data Retention

We retain gameplay data and account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Void Dominion is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the Game or on our website. Your continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
