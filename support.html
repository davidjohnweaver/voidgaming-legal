Common Questions

How do I recover my progress on a new device?

Link your account from the in-game Profile menu. Once linked, your progress and any purchases are tied to your account and restore automatically when you sign in on another device.

I made a purchase but didn't receive it.

Purchases are normally granted within a few moments. If an item hasn't appeared, fully close and reopen the app so it can re-validate your receipt. If it still hasn't arrived, contact us at the email above with your purchase details and we'll resolve it.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a request. If you have questions, reach out to us and we'll help where we can.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. On your device, open Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel.

How do I delete my account or data?

See our data deletion page for instructions, or email us and we'll process your request.
